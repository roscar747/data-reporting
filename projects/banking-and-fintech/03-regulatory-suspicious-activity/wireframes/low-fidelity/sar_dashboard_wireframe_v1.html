Data Dictionary Table: regulatory_sar_stubs_2023_2026 · 5,000 rows · 15 columns 9 Measures · Display Folder: Essential 8 | SAR Compliance KPIs
Columns (15)
sar_id Text Unique SAR identifier (e.g. SAR-2023-11330). Primary key for each stub.
filing_date Date Date the SAR was filed or is due to be filed. Used in M1, M7, M7b, trend charts.
regulatory_agency Text Regulator receiving the SAR: NCA (UK) or FinCEN (US). Top-level slicer dimension.
reporting_institution Text Institution submitting the SAR (e.g. Apex Fintech, Global Bank Corp). Used in velocity segmentation.
subject_type Text Individual or Entity. 50/50 split. Drives M6 heatmap rows and donut visual.
subject_occupation_industry Text Industry of the subject (e.g. Retail). Dimension for drill-through analysis.
activity_start_date Date Date suspicious activity began. Used as stub creation proxy in M5 velocity.
activity_end_date Date Date activity ended. 30-day deadline = activity_end_date + 30 days. Core to M1.
total_amount_involved Decimal Total financial value of suspicious activity. Used in M4 (priority exposure) and M6 (risk score).
currency Text Currency of transaction: GBP or USD. Slicer for multi-currency views.
suspicion_category Text 9 typologies (e.g. Structuring, Human Trafficking). Primary dimension for M2 treemap and bar chart.
is_urgent_filing Boolean TRUE = marked urgent by compliance officer. Filters M4 priority exposure value.
disposition_status Text Pipeline stage: Closed-Filed · Under Investigation · Referred to Law Enforcement. Drives M3 funnel.
narrative_summary_stub Text Free-text narrative summary. Presence/absence scores in M8 (FQS). Not used in aggregations.
associated_account_count Integer Number of accounts linked to the SAR subject. Used in M6 network breadth component.
Measures — Essential 8 | SAR Compliance KPIs (9 measures)
M1 | SAR Filing Backlog (Days to Deadline)COUNT of Under Investigation SARs where activity_end_date + 30 − TODAY() ≤ 10. Red KPI card.
M2 | Suspicion Concentration Index (%)Category % share of total SARs in context. Drives treemap and bar chart via cross-filter.
M3 | Stub-to-Filing Conversion Rate (%)Closed-Filed ÷ All stubs. Donut centre label and pipeline funnel fill widths.
M4 | Priority Exposure Value (£)SUM(total_amount_involved) WHERE is_urgent_filing = TRUE. Red KPI card + drillthrough.
M5 | Narrative Completion Velocity (Avg Days)AVERAGEX of DATEDIFF(activity_start_date, activity_end_date) for Closed-Filed stubs.
M6 | Regulatory Risk Heatmap ScoreComposite index = (Amount Index × 0.7) + (Account Index × 0.3) × 100. Matrix background colours.
M7 | Historical SAR Volume (2023-2025)COUNTROWS filtered YEAR(filing_date) 2023–2025. Historical line series on trend chart.
M7b | 2026 Projected SAR VolumeAnnualised 2026 YTD ÷ days elapsed × 365. Dashed projection line on trend chart.
M8 | Filing Quality Score - FQS (%)6-field completeness score per record: sar_id, filing_date, suspicion_category, subject_type, narrative_summary_stub, total_amount_involved.
